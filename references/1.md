## About the role
Familieretshuset handles major life transitions for citizens — divorce, custody, marriage, guardianship — running ~50,000 annual meetings. The Data & Analysis team (10 people) sits within Center for IT og Data alongside IT development and IT operations. The team is currently establishing an ML/AI infrastructure to put models safely into production, and they want this hire to be part of that journey.

Day-to-day tasks include:
- Collaborating with the business to uncover data needs and develop tailored solutions
- Building a technical setup as a foundation for classical ML and deep learning
- Ensuring data handling and storage complies with applicable standards and legislation
- Over time: developing, operating, and maintaining ML/deep learning models to generate insight and automate processes
- Translating data into concrete optimisation initiatives
